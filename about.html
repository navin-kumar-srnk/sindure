ABOUT

Let’s Grow Together!

With decades of experience we cater to the needs of the major players of the Automotive and Non-Automotive industry including the tier-1 companies of the Original Equipment manufacturing companies. We believe in the perennial mutual trust whom we work with, which made us retain their trust for decades as we have satisfied them with consistent quality, relentless support, and overwhelming quality.

Our state-of-the-art facility, use of the latest technology, highly skilled engineers and man-power helped us manufacture the net-shaped and near-net-shaped cold forged and machine components with quality and precision. Our proven and trusted methods of manufacturing have made us manufacture machine components with higher directional gain flow (Anisotropy) rendering maximum part strength facilitating better fatigue resistance and impact toughness on part sections, such as gear teeth.

The components we produce come with high close dimensional tolerances and good surface finish quality which makes even the industry majors trust us. Over and above the impeccable quality of our products, consistency, customer-centricity, and cost-effectiveness helps our customers repose their trust for decades.

Leadership Team

Basavaraj G Sindhur
Group Chairman

With 40 years of experience, Mr. Sindur is a visionary leader with a passion for taking the cold forging industry to the next level.
