Annealing

Annealing is a heat treatment process used to increase the ductility and reduce the hardness of a material. This change in hardness and ductility is a result of the reduction of dislocations in the crystal structure of the material being annealed. Annealing is performed in cold working process to prevent it from brittle failure or to make it more formable for subsequent operations.
